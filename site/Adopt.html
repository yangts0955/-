About adoption

Notice!

In order to prevent these poor animals from being abandoned again and to ensure that you won't be troubled by the fact that your adopted pet does not meet your expectations, we need you to provide some proofs before you can adopt. We will give some suggestions according them as well.
As follows:

  • 1.How many people are there in your house
  • 2.The size of the room
  • 3.If the child/children live with you
  • 4.Which floor do you live
  • 5.The hours of your working
  • 6.The expected value of the monthly average pet consumption
